How long does it take to build a custom dream house?

Building a custom home can vary based on the design, size, and complexity, but typically it takes between 8 to 12 months. Our team provides a detailed timeline after the initial design phase.

What is the average cost per square foot for building a custom home?

Costs can vary significantly depending on location, materials, and design choices. On average, custom home construction costs range from $150 to $300 per square foot. We offer a detailed cost breakdown during our consultation process.

Do I need to have my own land to build a dream house?

Not necessarily. We can help you find suitable land, or if you already own property, we'll work with you to assess its suitability and handle any site-specific considerations.

Can I make changes to the design during construction?

Yes, we offer flexibility to make certain changes during construction. However, some changes may affect the timeline and budget, which we'll discuss with you if adjustments are requested.
